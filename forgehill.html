Forgehill

Forgehill is a town nestled in the shadow of the Aldheimr Peaks and the edge of the great forest called the forest of whispers. It is a rugged town known for its exceptional craftsmanship and its blacksmithing heritage. The town is surrounded by dense forests, rolling foothills, a giant statue of unknown origin, and an abandoned mine steeped in superstition. The air is often filled with the sound of hammers on anvils, a testament to the tireless work of its residents.

You haven't heard much from Forgehill lately. Rumors, and news are often spilling from the Capitol through these hills, but in recent weeks the news has dwindled and fewer tradesmen have come down the road.

Forgehill's culture revolves around its smithing forges, the nearby forests, and a strong sense of community. The town's unique blend of artistry and resilience makes it a significant part of the region.

Points of Interest

The nearby Blackroot Thicket supplies the town with timber and holds many mysteries. Tales of strange whispers and shadowy figures circulate among the townsfolk, giving the forest an ominous reputation.

Forgehill is also home to a logging camp managed by Barriston Wren, which provides much-needed timber but faces its own share of dangers from the deeper forest.
