Welcome to Divide of Argaloth

This is the land of Argaloth. The current year is 4963 AFC (After the Forth Calamity). The 7th of September the Season of Harvestwind.

This site is dedicated to the world of Argaloth. Here you will find information on the world, its inhabitants, and the adventures that take place within it. This site is a work in progress and will be updated as more information is added.

The world of Argaloth is a high fantasy world filled with magic, mystery, and adventure. The world is divided into several regions, each with its own unique culture, history, and inhabitants. The world is home to a wide variety of creatures, from the noble elves to the fierce minotaurs. The world is also home to powerful magic, ancient ruins, and dark secrets waiting to be uncovered.

I am working on this site as a way to organize and share the information I have created for my Dungeons and Dragons campaign. I hope to create a resource that my players can use to learn more about the world and its inhabitants, as well as a place where I can keep track of the information I have created. I also hope to use this site as a way to share my love of storytelling and world building with others.

Most of the images I used here were created using chat GPT. I am not an artist, but I used what tools I had available to me.
