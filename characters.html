Characters of Anferdale

Rydell Stonegust

Town Elder

Race: Half-Elf

Age: Venerable, looks to be at least in his late sixties

Appearance: Long, silver-white hair; deep, serene green eyes; weathered skin with lines of wisdom; lean, slightly muscular build; dressed in robes of earthy tones adorned with intricate embroidery of leaves and woodland creatures; wears a pendant made from polished driftwood and a gnarled staff with ancient runes.

Personality: A thin, calm voice with an undertone of authority. Deliberate and fluid in movement, exuding wisdom and a deep connection to nature. Known for being wise, patient, nurturing, and hopeful.

Skills and Abilities: Deep knowledge of history, lore, and the natural world. Proficient in druidic nature magic, especially in knowledge and communication with nature. Acts as a mentor to the younger generation.

Role in Community: Temple caretaker, scholar, and guide. Tends to the town's small temple and gardens, preserves knowledge, and provides counsel to the people of Anferdale.

Backstory: Rydell has spent his life in harmony with nature, bridging the gap between human and elvish communities. He's become a pillar of Anferdale, respected for his unwavering dedication to the town and its land.

Interactions: Participates in town council meetings, provides sage advice, mentors the younger generation, and is a constant source of comfort and wisdom for the town.

Ima Adalhaid

Town Healer and Spiritual Guide

Race: Wood-Elf

Age: Ageless, appearing in her early-middle years

Appearance: Stands at a lithe 5'8" with magnolia-colored skin, long chestnut hair in braids with colorful beads and feathers, and piercing emerald eyes. She wears a moss-green robe belted with woven leaves and vines and carries a satchel of healing supplies. Around her neck is a leaf-shaped pendant gifted by forest spirits.

Personality: Soothing and melodic voice with a hint of an Elvish accent. Calm and graceful in demeanor, bringing peace and comfort. Known for being kind, nurturing, and wise.

Skills and Abilities: Extensive knowledge of medicinal plants and natural remedies. Proficient in healing magic focused on easing suffering and aiding the sick. Offers spiritual guidance and creates a sanctuary at the town temple.

Role in Community: Town healer and religious leader. Tends to the temple, performs rituals, and sits on the town council. Known for her gentle care and dedication to the sick and the community’s well-being.

Backstory: Ima’s connection to nature and healing began early, influenced by her Wood Elf heritage and a compassion for all life. She became a cherished figure in Anferdale, respected for her dual roles as healer and spiritual guide.

Interactions: Often seen offering gentle words of comfort and herbal remedies around town. Actively participates in council meetings and creates herbal charms during festivals, making her an essential figure in the community.

Harlan Ashhide

Town Guard Captain

Race: Half-Elf

Age: Early forties

Appearance: A tall, sturdy man with graying hair at the temples, a neatly trimmed beard, and piercing blue eyes. His slightly pointed ears and sharp features hint at his elvish heritage. Dressed in well-worn leather armor similar to his subordinates, his posture and demeanor command respect.

Personality: Friendly and approachable but takes his duties seriously. Known for his sense of duty and his serious demeanor, even if he seems to have misplaced his sense of humor. Vigilant and protective, particularly during the festival.

Skills and Abilities: Proficient in combat and crowd control, with a strong understanding of tactics and strategy. Skilled in mentoring and has a deep sense of duty toward the town’s well-being.

Role in Community: Serves as the town guard captain, responsible for training and mentoring younger guards and keeping the town safe. Known for his steady, reliable presence.

Backstory: Harlan grew up in Anferdale and left as a teenager, returning years later with a harder edge. He followed in his father's footsteps by joining the town guard, ultimately becoming its captain. He has served as a mentor to younger guards, instilling in them the values of duty and community.

Interactions: Seen frequently around town, patrolling or advising the townsfolk. Known for being approachable yet stern, providing a sense of security to the people of Anferdale.

Maris Deepwater

Head of Town Docks

Race: Elf

Age: Mid-thirties

Appearance: Slender and athletic with fair skin and a slight golden hue, long dark hair often tied back in a braid, and striking green eyes. She wears practical clothing suited for life on the water, often adorned with small charms related to her trade. Her ears have a subtle elvin point, and she has a confident, commanding presence.

Personality: Confident and commanding, with a strong sense of duty to her family’s fishing trade. Maris is known for her resilience and quick thinking on the water, balancing a friendly rapport with the crew and a professional seriousness about her work.

Skills and Abilities: Highly skilled in sailing, fishing, and navigation. Her agility and strength allow her to work efficiently on the lake, and she has knowledge of fishing routes, seasonal changes, and water trade practices.

Role in Community: Heads the Deepwater family and manages most of Anferdale’s fishing and water trade. Known for her dedication to her family’s legacy and her respected role within the town’s economy.

Backstory: Maris was raised in Anferdale, inheriting her family’s passion for fishing and trade. From a young age, she learned the skills of the trade, working alongside her family and eventually taking the helm of their operations. Her elvin heritage connects her with the natural rhythms of the lake, which she deeply respects.

Interactions: Maris is often seen on the docks or out on the water, overseeing fishing operations and working with the crew. She has a rapport with other townsfolk, particularly those involved in trade and logistics, and she plays an active role in the community council, advocating for the town’s prosperity.

Arlen Greenfield

Head of The Biggest Farming Family in Town

Race: Human

Age: Early Fifties

Appearance: Tall and robust with a muscular build from years of hard labor. Sun-tanned skin, short-cropped brown hair, and piercing blue eyes. He wears simple, practical clothing for farm work and sturdy boots. His hands are calloused, and his face is weathered from a life spent outdoors.

Personality: Down-to-earth and hardworking, with a strong sense of duty to his family and farm. Arlen is practical, determined, and values hard work. He’s known for his no-nonsense attitude and can be a bit stubborn but is ultimately fair-minded and loyal.

Skills and Abilities: Skilled in agriculture, crop management, and animal husbandry. Arlen has extensive knowledge of the land and its cycles, making him a key figure in Anferdale's farming community. His strength and endurance allow him to handle demanding tasks with ease.

Role in Community: Head of the Greenfield family, which oversees most of Anferdale’s farming trade. Arlen is deeply committed to ensuring the prosperity of his family and the town, often involved in community decisions that impact the agricultural economy.

Backstory:Arlen was born and raised in Anferdale, working on the family farm from a young age. Over the years, he took on greater responsibilities, eventually managing the farm and expanding its influence. His dedication to farming has earned him respect and made his family one of the wealthiest in town.

Interactions: Frequently seen working in the fields or overseeing trade in the marketplace. Arlen has strong connections within the town council and other trade-related families. His relationships with other families are sometimes competitive, especially with those in the fishing trade.

Suidbert Goodsong

Owner of The Riverside Mercantile

Race: Halfling

Age: Late Twenties

Appearance: Tanned leather-colored skin, stands atop a stool to see over the counter. Short, neatly styled dark brown hair, bright mischievous eyes full of humor. Wears fine attire: a purple jerkin with gleaming buttons, dark leather pants, and matching hide boots. Often seen with a small, ornate magnifying glass for inspecting goods.

Personality: Cheerful and robust, often laughing. Friendly and outgoing, known for bawdy jokes and a shrewd business sense. A sharp-witted merchant with a love for rare items and a knack for making people feel welcome.

Skills and Abilities: Expert negotiator, highly skilled in appraising goods for value and authenticity. Manages his shop and employees with a lighthearted, authoritative approach.

Role in Community: Runs The Riverside Mercantile, providing adventurers and townsfolk with both essential and rare items. A central hub for local gossip and news. Known for his generosity in local events and festivals.

Backstory: Grew up in the trade town of Talida's Crossing, learning the art of commerce from his family, who were traders. Moved to Anferdale to set up shop, attracted by the town’s unique location and clientele. Built a reputation for carrying diverse inventory and being a trusted appraiser of valuable goods.

Interactions: With customers, he is welcoming and humorous, a favorite among locals and newcomers. With employees, he fosters a positive, lighthearted work environment. Actively participates in local festivals, often donating goods from his store as prizes.

Reese Harper

Innkeeper of the Reaver's Rest

Race: Human

Age: Middle-Aged, Around 45

Appearance: Short salt-and-pepper hair, neatly combed; warm brown eyes with a hint of mischief and wisdom. Lightly tanned skin with smile lines around the eyes and mouth. Sturdy and slightly stocky build, dressed in a white linen shirt, brown leather apron, and dark trousers. Always has a cloth tucked into his apron for wiping mugs and spills.

Personality: Friendly and welcoming, known for his deep, hearty voice. Warm and hospitable, with a knack for making everyone feel at home. Charismatic and good-natured, he’s attentive to his guests’ needs and is well-liked by the community.

Skills and Abilities: Exceptional in hospitality, ensuring guests are comfortable and satisfied. Skilled cook, famous for the inn’s roasted potatoes and ales. Efficient at managing the bar, mixing drinks, and creating a lively atmosphere.

Role in Community: Runs The Reaver’s Rest, providing lodging and meals for travelers and locals. Acts as a community hub, often hosting local events and celebrations. A key figure in Anferdale’s social scene, hearing and sharing news from all corners of the town.

Backstory: Raised in Anferdale, Reese inherited The Reaver’s Rest from his parents and has dedicated his life to maintaining its welcoming reputation. Known for his generosity and excellent ale, he is a beloved figure in the community, always ready to lend a helping hand.

Interactions: Greets guests with a smile, often remembering regulars’ favorite drinks and meals. Runs the inn with a mix of friendliness and efficiency, respected by his staff for his high standards. Actively involved in local events, Reese’s knowledge of town gossip and news makes him a valuable resource.

Hilda Stonebrew

Barkeep of the Thirsty Thresher

Race: Half-Orc

Age: Late thirties

Appearance: Unusually tall, standing around 6'5\" to 6'8\" with a muscular, robust build. She has a ruddy, sun-kissed complexion and a broad, friendly face with small tusks that make her smile both fierce and welcoming. Her thick, dark hair is often tied back in a braid or ponytail. Wears a well-worn, ale-damp apron over sturdy clothes and boots fit for heavy work.

Personality: Jovial and always ready with a hearty laugh and a joke, making her popular among patrons. Despite her size, she’s skilled and agile in her work, and known for her friendly, welcoming demeanor. She has a natural charisma that draws people to the Thirsty Thresher.

Skills and Abilities: Highly skilled in bartending and customer service, with a strong reputation for efficient, friendly service. Known for her strength, but also her gentle touch and ability to handle delicate tasks. Makes patrons feel welcome and at home.

Role in Community: Runs the Thirsty Thresher, a favorite spot for both locals and travelers to unwind. Her establishment serves as a lively gathering place, and she’s seen as a beloved figure in the community.

Backstory: Hilda grew up in the mountains near Jotun's Way among her half-orc kin. After her father passed in a tribal skirmish, her mother settled in a human village when Hilda was seven. She loved village life, and over the years, she honed her skills and dreamt of creating a place where people of all kinds could gather and relax. Eventually, she opened the Thirsty Thresher, fulfilling her dream.

Interactions: Known for her strength and agility, she efficiently manages the tavern, moving heavy barrels and kegs with ease. She’s friendly with all patrons and often participates in local events. Hilda’s laugh and warmth make her a cherished figure in Anferdale.

Captain Mira Selarin

Captain of the Glimmerwind

Race: Water Genasi

Age: Mid-Fourties

Appearance: Mira is a striking figure, her blue-green skin carrying a faint luminescent sheen that reflects the light in a way that always seems slightly otherworldly. Her eyes, a brilliant shade of sea foam green, are perpetually alert, often scanning the horizon. Mira’s short, dark teal hair is swept back and often wet, giving her an effortlessly windswept appearance. She wears practical studded leather armor that’s seen years of use, and her favored short bow rests at her side, ready for action.

Personality: Mira is level-headed, observant, and reserved. She’s known for her calm under pressure and her ability to make quick, sound decisions, which has earned her the trust of her crew. While she’s generally quiet and direct, Mira is fiercely protective of those she cares about and will risk herself without hesitation if it means protecting her ship or her crew. She possesses a dry sense of humor that she only shares with those closest to her, and her gaze holds an intensity that speaks to her inner resolve.

Skills and Abilities: Mira is a natural navigator, with a strong knowledge of currents, weather patterns, and survival techniques. Her Water Genasi heritage allows her to stay calm and capable underwater, making her highly effective in both river and coastal environments. In addition to her navigational prowess, Mira is skilled in tactics and guerrilla-style combat, able to leverage her Swarmkeeper abilities to control a situation or create strategic diversions.

Role in Community: As the captain of the Glimmerwind Mira is not really a part of the community. Her only ties here are her relatively close friendships with the Deepwater family. And her close working relationship with Hilda.

Backstory: Mira’s family operated a modest trade fleet along the coast, and she spent her childhood learning the skills needed to navigate and survive on the open water. When her family lost their business due to a political dispute, Mira struck out on her own, using her skills to captain various vessels until she could afford her own. The *Glimmerwind* is the culmination of her hard work and dedication, and she’s fiercely protective of it and its crew. Mira’s reputation as a skilled and fair captain has made her well-respected among river traders and feared among bandits.

Interactions: Mira’s sloop, the *Glimmerwind*, is both her pride and her sanctuary. It represents her life’s work and holds immense sentimental value. She oversees its maintenance personally, ensuring everything is in working order, from the rigging to the ballistae. Her crew knows that even the smallest crack or frayed rope will be met with her swift attention, and slacking on maintenance is one of the few things that can provoke her temper. The ship is fast and agile, ideal for trade along the river and coast, and it’s been specially reinforced for defense against bandit attacks.

Agatha

Serving Girl at the Reaver's Rest

Race: Half-Elf

Age: Early Twenties

Appearance: Agatha is a radiant young woman with auburn hair that falls in loose curls around her shoulders and warm hazel eyes that sparkle with quiet confidence. She has a petite yet graceful frame, often seen in simple but well-kept serving attire. Her smile is infectious, and she often displays it while she works.

Personality: Kind-hearted and thoughtful, Agatha is a comforting presence at the Reaver's Rest. She has a gentle humor and a sharp wit that make her a favorite among patrons. While outwardly cheerful, she harbors a deep longing for adventure and often dreams of life beyond Anferdale. Her loyalty to those she cares about is unwavering, and she is known to stand her ground when necessary.

Skills and Abilities: Agatha has a natural talent for reading people, often knowing what a patron needs before they do. She’s quick on her feet and has an excellent memory, making her invaluable in the bustling Reaver's Rest. Her knack for diffusing tense situations has earned her respect among the locals.

Role in Community: As a serving girl at the Reaver’s Rest, Agatha is well-connected and acts as an unofficial confidant to many in the town. Her warm demeanor and genuine interest in others’ lives make her a central figure in the tavern’s lively atmosphere.

Backstory: Agatha grew up in Anferdale, spending much of her childhood around the Reaver’s Rest. Her parents passed away when Agatha was young, leaving her to help run the family business with her uncle. Despite her small-town roots, Agatha has always been curious about the world beyond the lake and harbors a quiet ambition to explore it someday.

Brom Vernell

Blacksmith of Anferdale

Race: Human

Age: Early Thirties

Appearance: Brom is a tall, muscular man with jet-black hair and a perpetual smirk that gives him an air of mischief. His skin is deep bronze from countless hours at the forge, and his green eyes sparkle with energy and enthusiasm. He’s almost always covered in soot or wearing a heavy leather apron, a testament to his dedication to his craft.

Personality: Brom is lively, boisterous, and full of charm, making him the life of the party in any gathering. He has a quick wit and an infectious laugh that draws people in, but beneath his outgoing nature lies a serious passion for his work. He is loyal to his friends and community and always willing to lend a hand or a good story.

Skills and Abilities: Brom is a master blacksmith, capable of crafting weapons, armor, and intricate decorative pieces that reflect the influences of his travels. He is also an accomplished archer, often competing in local contests where his natural talent shines. As a Fighter, he combines his skills with discipline, making him a formidable protector of his community.

Role in Community: As the blacksmith of Anferdale, Brom is an essential figure in town life, providing tools, repairs, and equipment for locals and travelers alike. His forge, Ironfist Forge, is a hub of activity where people come not just for his work but also for his lively personality and camaraderie.

Backstory: Brom grew up in a family of renowned blacksmiths with mixed heritage, learning the art of smithing from his father, who traveled widely to master his craft. Inspired by these stories, Brom traveled in his younger years, honing his own skills before settling in Anferdale. He saw the town’s potential and was drawn by the chance to build his own legacy. Brom’s sister, Elara, occasionally helps him with the forge, though she spends most of her time elsewhere in town.

Interactions: Brom is a constant presence in Anferdale, known for his booming laugh and hearty greetings. Whether chatting with patrons at the Thirsty Thresher or showing off his archery skills at the festival, Brom is always engaged with his community. His friendship with Thrain Oakenfield, though often marked by their contrasting personalities, is one of mutual respect and shared expertise.

Thrain Oakenfield

Carpenter of Anferdale

Race: Dwarf

Age: 152

Appearance: Thrain is a stocky, muscular dwarf with strong, calloused hands that bear the marks of years spent working with wood and metal. His deep auburn hair, streaked with gray, is tied back in a neat braid, and his beard, adorned with small iron and gold beads, reaches his chest. He wears a sturdy leather apron over durable clothes, often covered in sawdust from his work.

Personality: Quiet and reserved, Thrain lets his work speak for itself. He is meticulous and patient, with a deep respect for craftsmanship and tradition. Though he rarely shows emotion, his steady demeanor and occasional twinkle in his eye reveal his pride in his craft and subtle warmth toward those he cares about.

Skills and Abilities: Thrain is a master carpenter, renowned for crafting everything from carts and wheels to ornate wooden sculptures. His precision in woodworking translates into his skill as an archer, making him a formidable hunter and competitor in local contests. As a Ranger, he excels in tracking, survival, and combat, particularly against undead.

Role in Community: Thrain’s workshop is an integral part of Anferdale, providing the town with essential tools, furniture, and repairs. Despite his reserved nature, he is well-liked and respected, often humming old dwarven tunes as he works. He is a dependable figure in the town, maintaining strong friendships with Brom Vernell and Hilda Stonebrew.

Backstory: Thrain comes from a long line of craftsmen, though he chose woodworking over his family’s traditional blacksmithing. This decision initially disappointed his father, but Thrain’s skill and dedication eventually won his family’s pride. He developed his archery skills during his youth while assisting his parents in the woods, honing his precision and patience. Now, he channels these skills into both his craft and his role as a protector of the town.

Interactions: Thrain is a quiet but steadfast presence in the community. He frequently collaborates with Brom Vernell on projects and enjoys unwinding at the Thirsty Thresher, though he prefers listening to others’ stories over sharing his own. His occasional gifts of intricately carved wooden tokens are cherished by friends and neighbors alike.

Rowan Gale

Hermit of Anferdale

Race: Human

Age: Late 60s

Appearance: Rowan is a wiry, weathered man with gray-streaked brown hair tied loosely at the nape of his neck. His face is lined with age, and his hazel eyes hold both the weight of regret and the spark of wisdom. He wears simple, practical clothes in earthy tones, and his sturdy cloak smells faintly of pine. A polished oak staff doubles as his walking stick and a symbol of his faith. Despite his age, Rowan moves with a grace born of his connection to the natural world.

Personality: Rowan is soft-spoken and unassuming, offering wisdom through parables or gentle nudges rather than direct instruction. His humility often leads him to downplay his own abilities. When pressed, however, he can reveal flashes of resolve and insight, hinting at hidden depths. Though secretive, he remains compassionate and willing to aid those in need.

Skills and Abilities: Rowan is a master herbalist and survivalist, possessing a deep knowledge of plants, animals, and the land. His ability to read the weather and terrain is uncanny. Though his combat skills have diminished, his precision with a staff and his healing abilities remain formidable. Rowan’s divine connection to the natural world allows him to channel its power, though his confidence in his worthiness sometimes wavers.

Role in Community: Rowan is a reclusive figure who rarely ventures into Anferdale, yet he is respected as an elder and an authority on nature. Though he has refused a seat on the town council, his advice is sometimes sought in times of need. Many regard him as a mysterious guardian of the woods and its secrets.

Backstory: Little is known of Rowan’s past, but his deep knowledge of distant lands and intricate rituals hints at a life beyond the forests of Anferdale. He has lived alone for many years, tending to the forest and its creatures. Rowan’s solitary existence and reluctance to speak of his past only add to the air of mystery surrounding him.

Interactions: Rowan is slow to trust, but those who earn his confidence find a wealth of wisdom and guidance. His practical knowledge of survival and nature is invaluable, and he can serve as a mentor or ally for those who respect the natural world. When asked about his past he often simply refuses to speak.

Eoric Greenfield

Eldest Son of the Greenfield Family

Race: Human

Age: Late Twenties

Appearance: Eoric is a tall, lean man with sharp features and meticulously styled dark brown hair. His piercing hazel eyes are often narrowed in disdain, and his finely tailored clothing reflects his family’s wealth. He often wears studded leather armor polished to a shine, more decorative than practical.

Personality: Arrogant and entitled, Eoric believes his family’s wealth and status place him above the common folk of Anferdale. He has a quick tongue for insults and an inflated view of his own abilities. While outwardly charming when it suits him, his disdain for others often slips through, especially when he feels slighted.

Skills and Abilities: Eoric’s archery skills are the product of extensive private tutoring arranged by his father. However, his lack of discipline and overconfidence prevent him from reaching his full potential. He is skilled in persuasion and deception, often manipulating others to his advantage.

Role in Community: As the eldest son of Arlen Greenfield, Eoric represents his family in public events and competitions, often flaunting his wealth and privilege. Though disliked by many, his influence is undeniable, and few dare to openly challenge him.

Backstory: Eoric grew up in the lap of luxury, never wanting for anything. His father ensured he received the finest education and training, hoping to mold him into a capable heir. However, Eoric’s arrogance and lack of work ethic have made him more of a liability than an asset. His father’s frequent interference, including bribing competitors and judges, has only reinforced Eoric’s belief in his own superiority.

Interactions: Eoric is a frequent participant in local archery contests, where his inflated ego and underhanded tactics often create tension. He is quick to boast of his wealth and status, especially when trying to gain favor or intimidate others. While he often clashes with people in town, his position as Arlen Greenfield’s heir ensures he is tolerated—at least, for now.
